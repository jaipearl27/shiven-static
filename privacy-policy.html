Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how shiven collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Financial Consultant CRM ("Platform").

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, professional details.
  • Client Data: Financial profiles, goals, income, investment details, and notes.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, access times, and activity logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and manage your access to the Platform.
  • To store and organize your client records securely.
  • To send system notifications, updates, and support communications.
  • To analyze usage and improve user experience and CRM features.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission.
  • Data is encrypted at rest on secure cloud servers.
  • Role-based access controls and user authentication.
  • Regular vulnerability scans and system audits.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your data. Your information may be shared only:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce our policies.
  • With trusted service providers who support our CRM, under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide services, resolve disputes, or meet legal requirements.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data.
  • Update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal or client data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Platform implies acceptance of the revised policy.
