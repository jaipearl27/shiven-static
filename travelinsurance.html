What Is Travel Insurance?

Travel insurance is a specialised policy that covers risks associated with domestic or international travel. It helps protect travellers against trip disruptions, lost baggage, medical emergencies, flight delays, and other unforeseen situations while travelling. For travellers, it brings peace of mind. For agents, it’s a growing opportunity. With Shiven CRM, advisors can efficiently manage client data, compare policies, automate renewals, and deliver excellent service from start to finish.

Common Types of Travel Insurance

Different businesses have varying needs for coverage based on size, industry, and risk profile. Shiven CRM enables your team to suggest and administer the appropriate plan for each client.

1. Single Trip Insurance

Covers one trip typically includes flight delay, trip cancellation, medical expenses, and lost luggage. Best for leisure or business travellers taking one-time trips

2. Multi-Trip Insurance

Offers year-round coverage for frequent travellers with multiple trips under one policy. Ideal for corporate clients, frequent flyers, and business executives

3. Student Travel Insurance

Designed for students studying abroad, this plan covers healthcare, tuition interruption, passport loss, and sponsor protection. Highly recommended for international students heading to universities or colleges

4. Family Travel Insurance

Protects an entire family under one plan when travelling together. Covers medical expenses, baggage loss, and emergency return across all insured members

5. Senior Citizen Travel Plans

Tailored plans for older travelers, offering enhanced medical benefits, coverage for pre-existing conditions, and easy claims. Suitable for international travel, vacations, or visits to family overseas

Add-Ons for Travel Insurance

Travel insurance can be enhanced with optional add-ons, helping clients travel with greater confidence. Shiven CRM makes it simple to offer these add-ons at the right time:

      • Adventure Sports Cover: Ideal for clients participating in skiing, scuba diving, or other activities
      • Flight Delay/Cancellation Benefits: Offers compensation for extended delays or missed connections
      • Baggage Delay Cover: Provides reimbursement for essentials in case of baggage delay
      • Baggage Loss Cover: Offers compensation for permanently lost or stolen checked-in baggage
      • Credit Card Loss Cover: Covers misuse and fraudulent charges in case of lost or stolen credit cards
      • Financial Loss Cover: Reimburses non-refundable expenses due to cancellations or emergencies
      • Companion Cover: Supports travel companions in case of emergency hospitalization

Add-ons are a great way to personalize policies and increase policy value, all while giving clients more comprehensive protection.
