What is a Mutual funds CRM?

A Mutual Fund Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system is a robust software solution built exclusively for mutual fund advisors, distributors, and financial consultants to handle investor relations, automate operational processes, and ensure regulatory compliance. Right from lead nurturing and tracking of KYC to SIP reminders and portfolio monitoring, a Mutual Fund CRM consolidates all the important investor details, allowing professionals to offer efficient, customised, and value-oriented financial advisory services.
