Automotive Loans

What is an Automotive Loan?

An automobile loan, also called a car or vehicle loan, is a secured loan offered by financial institutions or banks to enable a person to buy a vehicle, new or old. The loan is generally paid in equated monthly instalments (EMIs) over a duration, and the vehicle serves as security untilrepayment of the loan in full. These loans are popular due to their affordability and repayment choices, allowing individuals to own a car without forking out an initial lump sum of money.

For loan consultants and advisors dealing with dozens of hundreds of such loans, it may be tough to stay organised and effective. That is where Shiven Infotech Automotive Loan CRM steps in.
