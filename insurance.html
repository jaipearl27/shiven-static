What is an Insurance CRM?

An Insurance Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system is a specialised software bundle that facilitates insurance companies, brokers, and agents in managing their customers effectively. From policy administration and lead monitoring to communication automation and reports generated on data, an insurance CRM holds all the critical customer details in one place. It streamlines the entire customer life cycle, prospecting and onboarding, renewals, and claims, and allows businesses to grow and expand their customer base with ease.
