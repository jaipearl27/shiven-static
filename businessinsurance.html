What Is Business/Commercial Insurance?

Commercial or business insurance offers customized coverage to safeguard businesses, entrepreneurs, and business property owners from risks that may interfere with operations or result in financial loss. Such policies are available for most industries and business forms, from retail stores to manufacturing plants, transportation companies to IT businesses. It offers coverage against property damage, liability concerns, employee-related exposures, and other business risks. With Shiven CRM, complex commercial insurance plans are made easy, traceable, and completely automated.

Major Types of Business/Commercial Insurance

Different businesses have varying needs for coverage based on size, industry, and risk profile. Shiven CRM enables your team to suggest and administer the appropriate plan for each client.

1. Property Insurance

Covers commercial buildings, office space, inventory, equipment, and other properties from occurrences such as fire, water damage, or structural flaws.

2. Liability Insurance

A single policy that insures all family members, pooling a collective sum insured. Best for couples, parents, and children under a single premium

3. Workplace Injury Cover

Provides cover for medical costs, disability, or rehabilitation of staff in case there is an accident on business premises.

4. Business Interruption Insurance

Provides cover for loss of income while business activities are suspended for reasons such as fire, structural repairs, or external interruptions.

5. Cyber Insurance

Critical for companies with delivery or transportation fleets. Protects company-owned vehicles from damage, liabilities, and road hazards.

5. Cyber Insurance

Defending against data breaches, system attacks, and cyber fraud is a must for today's businesses handling customer data and digital systems.

Add-On Coverage Options

Companies frequently require specialized coverage beyond standard policies. Shiven CRM enables your team to recommend and manage add-ons like:

      • Equipment Breakdown Cover: Covers breakdown or replacement for essential machines and technical equipment
      • Money Insurance: Insures money on transit or kept in premises
      • Fidelity Guarantee: Insures against monetary loss by way of employee fraud
      • Stock Insurance: Offers protection for goods kept in transit or warehouses

With Shiven CRM, agents are able to offer these add-ons at the appropriate time during the sales or renewal process, optimizing policy value and customer satisfaction.
