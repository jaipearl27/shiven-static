What is a Business Loan?

A business loan is a credit instrument that is designed to provide funding to businesses for one purpose or another—whether it's starting a new business, scaling up operations, acquiring stock, or for managing liquidity. Business loans also vary from personal loans because they're designed to meet the particular needs of running and growing a business. Business loans may be secured and unsecured, and terms of repayment typically involve fixed monthly payments, interest, and fixed tenors according to lender policy.

For business leaders and entrepreneurs, access to the right loan is key to continued expansion and addressing problems of working capital. For financial advisors who assist businesses in securing loans, juggling these multiple and frequently complex loans can be more than it can handle in the absence of suitable tools.
