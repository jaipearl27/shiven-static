What is Personal Loans

Personal loans are non-secured personal loans given by banks and financial institutions to individuals for their own use. Personal loans are adjustable in nature and may be taken for personal loans, medical expenses, marriage, holidays, education, or home improvement. As they are not secured, personal loans are made available to different lenders.

Mostly, personal loans come with fixed interest rates and scheduled repayment periods, making it easy for the borrowers to calculate monthly payments. Their quick processing and flexible applications make personal loans a popular way of meeting sudden financial demands without drawing on savings.
