Services We Provide

At Shiven Infotech, we provide a Complete Financial CRM solution designed to help financial consultants efficiently manage their client relationships. Whether you offer services such as insurance, loans, mutual funds, or other financial products, our CRM system streamlines your operations, enhances client engagement, and supports business growth.

Insurance

Our insurance CRM streamlines customer interactions, policy management, and claims processing for brokers and agents. Tailored for health, travel, and auto insurance, it optimizes workflows from lead acquisition to policy renewal. The intuitive interface enhances agent-client communication and simplifies daily tasks.

Mutual Fund

Our CRM solution simplifies mutual fund client management, investment tracking, and compliance, enhancing advisor-client communication and streamlining daily operations.

Loans

For financial advisers, our loan management CRM simplifies customer interactions and loan procedures. It streamlines processes for corporate, home, and personal loans from application to payment. The user-friendly interface improves communication between advisors and clients while also simplifying daily duties.

Stock Exchange

Our CRM solution is tailored for stock brokers, streamlining client management, trade tracking, and compliance. Designed for equity, commodity, and fore markets, it optimizes workflows from lead acquisition to trade execution. The intuitive interface enhances broker-client communication and simplifies daily operations.
