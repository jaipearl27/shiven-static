What Is an Education Loan?

An education loan is a funding process that helps students and parents to fund the cost of education at a university. The money usually pays for the payment of a university tuition fee, books, accommodation, transport, and other miscellaneous fees. Education loans help students fund their education free from the burden by offering flexible repayment terms as well as low interest rates. Whether pursued in India or abroad, education loans have a significant role as a contributory factor towards students ' professional and personal growth.

Education Loan Types

Undergraduate Education Loans: Bachelor's degree in various streams.

Postgraduate Education Loans

Master's, MBA, and other higher studies.

Vocational and Skill Development Loans

Professional courses and technical certification programs.

Overseas Education Loans

To study abroad by funding tuition, lodging, airfare, and living costs

Professional Course Loans

For these courses, like medicine, law, engineering, and management.
